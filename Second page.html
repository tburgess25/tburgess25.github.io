Police

The Police Department is a very important part of public safety in a given community. Police are the law and order of a community and it is their job to protect people. Police are charged with making arrest's if someone has committed a crime, and investigating crimes that come to their attention.

To understand Police, We will be looking at some of the equipment police officers use.

This is a Police Car:

This is a police car. Also sometimes called a police cruiser. This tool is used by police to move around a certain areas. Police Officers spend most of their time patroling their jurisdictions in these vehicles. The vehicles are equipment like laptops, lights and sirens, and other life saving equipment.

This is a pair of Police Handcuffs:

These are a pair of police handcuffs. The purpose of this tool is to restrain anyone who is being taken into custody. These are an important tool for police to have, because they make it impossible for a suspect to fight or grab for anything that may hurt others, including the officer.
