Fire

Fire Fighters and Fire Departments are an important part of the saftey of a community. The main purpose for a fire department is to provide assistance in putting out fires (wether it be in a house, in the woods, or of a vehicle), Rescuing people from car accidents, and providing life saving first aid.

This is a Fire Truck

This is a Fire Truck. The primary purpose of a Fire Truck is to Transport fire fighters and their equipment to a scene of an emergency. These massive machines also have the ability to pump water through hoses at immensly high rates. This helps to put out fires quickly.

This is a Hose

This is a Fire Hose. This serves a very important purpose for Fire Fighters. This delivers water to fires at very high preassures. This connects to the fire engine pump and allows fire fighters to put water on fire very quickly to put it out.
