EMS

EMS workers are a vital part of public safety in a comnunity. The primary job of these highly trained individuals is to provides life saving medical help to people. These individuals are highly trained and see some of the hardest things in the field of public safety

This is an Ambulance

This is an Ambulance. This very specialized piece of equipment is equiped with state of the art life saving equipment. The type of equipment on this vehicle includes a stretcher, a computer that tells paramedics vital signs, and a life pac that can keep a patient alive.

This is a Stretcher

This is a stretcher. This piece of equipment is very important, because it allows for easy transportation of a patient. One of the most technologically advanced pieces of equipment that is a part of the ambulance, this device can carry people in the worst pain with a lot of comfort.
